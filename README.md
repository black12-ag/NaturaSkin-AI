# NaturaSkin AI

**Bring Real Life Back to AI Skin.**

NaturaSkin AI is a specialized tool designed to fix the "plastic/glossy" look often found in AI-generated portraits. By injecting realistic micropores, texture, and natural imperfections, it transforms artificial faces into authentic, human-like images.
